Services

We offer guided investment services for all genres of investors. We build your portfolio based on your needs. Portfolio building is made simple with our team of investors who have your best interests at heart.

Our expertise range from retirement investing to young people looking to grow their savings. However, our specialty is our aggressive investment model which is able to beat the S&P 500 by upwards of 3%.

Prices

Our pricing varies depending on the size of your portfolio. Our fees are based upon your earnings, we take a small percentage of unrealized gains from each quarter.

  • $100k or less, 1.00% of unrealized gains. Maximum of $200 per quarter.
  • $100k - $500k, 0.75% of unrealized gains. Maximum of $500 per quarter.
  • $500k or more, 0.50% of unrealized gains. Maximim of $1,000 per quarter.
